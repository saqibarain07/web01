About Toyota :

Indus Motor was incorporated as a public limited Company in December 1989 And started commerical production in May 1993. Shares of the company are qouted on the Pakistan Stock Exchange.

In March 2000, the company started production of Daihatsu Coure in Pakistan which was based on the domestic Mira and features an 850-cc engine

In 2008, Toyota acquired 9.83 million shares from Overseas Investors AG and general public at the price of Rs370 per share.

In January 2012, the company halted Coure production in Pakistan.

Former Chairman of Indus Motor Company Ltd, Ali Suleman Habib died on 18 April 2020.
