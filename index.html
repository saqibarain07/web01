Indus Motor Company Ltd.

Indus Motor Company Limited, Oprating As Toyota Indus, is a Pakistan automobile manufacturer which is a subsidiary of Japanese multinational automaker Toyota. It is a based in Karachi, Pakistan.

Founded in 1989, as a joint venture between House Of Habib, Toyota Tsusho and Toyota Motors.

Indus Motor is the authorized assembler and manufacturer of Toyota And Daihatsu vehicles, auto part and accessories in Pakistan since 1 July 1990 at its 105 acres (0.42 km2) manufacturing plant at Port Bin Qasim Industrial Zone, outside Karachi.
